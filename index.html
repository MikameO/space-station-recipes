NanoTrasen ChemDB is an unofficial community tool for Space Station 14, an open-source multiplayer game. All content — including reagent names, chemical formulas, synthesis recipes, toxicity ratings, delivery methods, and antagonist strategies — describes fictional in-game mechanics. Real chemical names are used by the game for flavor; the properties, reactions, and effects shown here do not reflect real-world chemistry. This site provides no real-world medical, chemical, or safety guidance.
